Tim Sutherland is an International Cinematographer's Guild Cinematographer and Steadicam Operator.

After graduating from Taylor University in December of 2007, he moved to Los Angeles to pursue a career in film and television and began working at a the camera rental house Birns and Sawyer in Hollywood.






Tim joined local 600 as a Digital Intermediate Technician in 2010. In 2013 he moved to Nashville, TN to work on the ABC show, â€œNashville" and moved up to Camera Operator on season 3.

On season 4, Tim became the A Camera and Steadicam Operator, as well as working as the DP on scout days. During season 6 he was the Director of Photography on 6 complete episodes.

Since Nashville ended, Tim has been busy working as a DP and Steadicam Operator on several features, commercials, music videos, and television shows.
